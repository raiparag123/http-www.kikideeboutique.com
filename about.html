About Us

KIKI DEE FOUNDERS,

"At Kiki Dee we strive to be different - we prefer styles that are fabric rich, with standout prints and indo-western styling, rather than the traditional heavily embroidered garments widely available in the market."

Kiki Dee Boutique was founded in January 2016 when aunt and niece team, Deepa and Nisha saw a gap in the market (after many unsuccessful shopping trips to Indian high street retailers) for Indian clothing with a western edge.

Kiki Dee Boutique brings you the latest in Ladies fashion and we pride ourselves on ‘guilt free spending’ bringing you Lehenga's, Anarkali's and Gown's that are fashionable yet affordable, so that you can look fabulous without spending a fortune.

We have been on a journey of growth thanks to the great response we have received from our clients. Kiki Dee started off solely offering ready-made garments which were available to buy only at Pop -Up exhibitions, however the business has successfully grown so we are now able to offer:

Kiki Dee Couture - a full range of ready to wear outfits designed exclusively by us and created by our team of highly talented designers in India.

Curve by Kiki Dee - we recognise that those with a fuller figure deserve the same fashion forward choices available in the wider market and as a result launched a plus size range in 2018. These garments have been carefully designed by choosing fabrics, colours and shapes that compliment the fuller figure.

Bespoke Service - If what you are looking for is not on our rails, we can create bespoke pieces to suit all budgets and requirements. The bespoke service starts from £150 onwards. Bespoke pieces are always personal to the client and the same design will not be repeated for anyone else, making is truly exclusive and that bit extra special!

Personal 121 shopping or bespoke order appointments – if shopping in hectic crowds is not for you, you can also shop the Kiki Dee range by using the appointment form to book a viewing appointment for ready-made garments or a consultation appointment for a bespoke order.

At Kiki Dee Boutique there really is something or everyone!
